Roosevelt

Roosevelt (born Marius Lauber on 29 September 1990) is a German singer, songwriter and producer from Viersen.

Intro

Marius Laubers first project was Beat!Beat!Beat!, which was an indie teenage band where he was the drummer. Years later the band separated and he became a soloist. He then released his acclaimed debut EP 'Elliot' in 2013. The lead single 'Elliot' was named as Pitchfork's 'Best New Track'. Roosevelt then released the double-A side 'Night Moves/Hold On' in 2015 on Greco-Roman, which continued a warm synth-pop, Balearic-influenced sound.

In April 2016, he announced his self-titled debut album, set for release on August 19, 2016 on Greco-Roman/City Slang, with the video for tracks "Colours" and "Moving On". He has remixed the likes of Glass Animals, Jax Jones, Truls, Sundara Karma, Luka Vasta and Kakkmaddafakka, and on tour has supported Hot Chip, Totally Enormous Extinct Dinosaurs and Crystal Fighters. In June 2018, he announced a second album, Young Romance. It was released on September 28, 2018. The first single from this album, "Under the Sun," was released on June 27, 2018.

Sea

Roosevelts very first release was an EP titled "Sea" in 2012, which really set the tone for his upcoming releases. This single centered around a funky bass riff, layered with smooth synth sounds and a complimentary vocal melody.

Roosevelt

Roosevelt's debut album was a cocktail of disco, French touch, Ibiza house, yacht rock, and electropop that evokes some crowded Tiki-torch dancefloor lost on the Mediterranean coast. It featrued hits such as "Fever", and "Colours/Moving On".

Young Romance

His 2016 debut was a pleasant enough collection of bittersweet, '80s-hued love songs that marked the indie musician turned DJ/producer as one to watch. Young Romance, his second outing, sees Marius raising the bar throughout in terms of production and overall tightness, while adding some funk-lite guitar work for good measure as well.
